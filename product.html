Extremely Useful Product Features

Customer Management System

Comprehensive customer management system from lead generation to customer satisfaction reporting. The system will guide you through each stage of a customer's journey.


Guiding you through will mean there are less errors on a customers file that need addressing.

Stock/Asset Control

Reliable inventory management, know what is available to you right now and when to expect stock returns


Be able to give a customer an answer in regards to stock availablity, alternatives to their original request or to give them dates of return. Increasing the retention rate of your customers.

Secure Payments

PCI DSS compliant payments system means your customers payment information can be trusted with you.


Take payments for initial hire, sales of a new item or a new rental and create an automated schedule for subsequent payments. Customer card details are securely encrypted and you are provided with a token to use.

Logistics Assistance

A multi faceted part of the system allowing the management of a delivery fleet, courier services, deliveries and collections.


Organise your fleet for optimal fuel consumption and save time on the road. If you only use courier services for your products to ensure than despatching products is done with the greatest of ease in a well thought out process.
